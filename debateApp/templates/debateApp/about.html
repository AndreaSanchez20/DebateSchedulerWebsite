Tournament Schedule Pal

During debate tournaments, there are multiple debate rounds that each registered team must attend.

So, if you are a debate student or judge, fear no more! This website organizea the rounds and results for both judges and students who are at the competition making it easier to find the details of the next round you must attend.

Judges are able to sign in and complete their ballots and students have access to find the details (when, where, team competing against) of their next round. Both or even the public have access to round details by going to the dashboard page and looking for the team they are following
